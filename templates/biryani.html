Veg Biryani 🍛

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • Biryani masala, yogurt, onions
  • Spices, oil, salt

How to Make:

Cook rice separately. Fry onions and spices, add vegetables and yogurt. Layer with rice and cook on low flame for 10 min.
