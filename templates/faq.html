General nutrition and diet


What is nutrition?


Nutrition is the process of consuming food and using it for energy, growth, and repair of the body.

What are the essential nutrients?

There are six major classes of nutrients: carbohydrates, proteins, fats, vitamins, minerals, and water.

Why is nutrition important?

Proper nutrition helps maintain good health, supports immunity, and reduces disease risk.

How do I eat healthfully?

Focus on unprocessed foods, such as fruits, vegetables, and whole grains, and cook meals at home to better control salt and preservatives.

What is NutriGuide and how does it work?

What are probiotics and prebiotics?

These are beneficial for gut health and are often found in fermented foods or supplements.

What are the best macros?

Macro tracking is a popular topic, and a balanced intake of protein, carbs, and fats is important.
