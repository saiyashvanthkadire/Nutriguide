How it works

A journey towards life... one step at a time

There are two ways you can start your journey to a healthier life – Either meet me in person or JOIN iPlan (Online Nutrition Program).

In Person NutriGuide iPlan

Nutrition Consulting

When you come for the first time, I will do a comprehensive analysis of your current lifestyle and wellbeing. Then, I will devise a practical and achievable holistic nutrition plan and workout prescription. My aim is not to lure you with short-term goals but to help you develop sustainable habits for life.

Relapse Prevention and Education Session

Finally, you are here - THE PROMISED LAND! You have achieved your goals. Now what? The Relapse Prevention and Education Session ensures you maintain this healthy balance for life.
