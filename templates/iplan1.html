NutriGuide iPlan

For those who want expert nutrition guidance from anywhere — comfort, flexibility, and results combined.

What is iPlan?

iPlan is a flexible online consultation model for those who can’t meet in person. We connect through calls or video chats instead, while you follow your personalized plan from the comfort of home.

How NutriGuide iPlan Works

Step 1 – Initial Assessment

You receive a detailed questionnaire to help us understand your lifestyle, preferences, and health goals.

Step 2 – Consultation

We connect via phone or video call to discuss your responses and clarify your needs.

Step 3 – Personalized Plan

Within 48 hours, you receive a customized nutrition and wellness plan tailored to your goals.

Step 4 – Follow-ups

Regular check-ins help track your progress and make necessary adjustments along the way.

Step 5 – Relapse Prevention

A final session equips you with the tools to sustain your progress for the long term.

Who Should Choose iPlan?

  • Busy professionals
  • Students living away from home
  • International clients
  • Remote-area residents
  • Anyone preferring virtual wellness

What Makes iPlan Unique?

  • Global Reach: Serving clients across continents with culturally sensitive plans.
  • Holistic Approach: Focus on nutrition, lifestyle, mental wellness, and habit-building.
  • Real-Time Support: Quick responses and adaptive planning.
  • Privacy & Comfort: Sessions are confidential and from your safe space.
