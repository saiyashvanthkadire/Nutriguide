Fresh Orange Juice 🍊

Ingredients:

  • 4 fresh oranges
  • 1 teaspoon honey (optional)
  • Ice cubes

How to Make:

Cut the oranges in half and squeeze out the juice using a juicer or manually. Strain the juice to remove pulp if you prefer. Add a teaspoon of honey if desired and stir well. Pour into a glass with ice cubes and enjoy your fresh orange juice!
