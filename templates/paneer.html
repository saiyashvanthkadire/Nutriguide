Paneer Butter Masala 🧀

Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer cubes
  • 2 tomatoes, 1 onion
  • Butter, cream, garam masala
  • Salt, chili powder

How to Make:

Blend onion-tomato. Sauté in butter, add spices, cream, and paneer. Simmer 10 minutes and serve hot.
