Greek Salad 🥗

Ingredients:

  • 1 cucumber (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • Feta cheese cubes
  • Olives, olive oil, salt, pepper

How to Make:

Mix cucumber, tomatoes, and onion. Add feta and olives. Drizzle olive oil, sprinkle salt and pepper, and serve fresh.
